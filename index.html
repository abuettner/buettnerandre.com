Heisann!

I am a cybersecurity researcher based in Oslo, Norway, with a particular interest in Web Security and User Authentication.

Currently, I work as postdoctoral fellow in the Digital Security Group at the University of Oslo. Here, I also did my Ph.D. with a focus on emerging authentication technologies. Before that, I received both my Bachelor's (B.Sc.) and Master's (M.Sc.) degree in the Media Technology study program at the TH Köln in Germany.
